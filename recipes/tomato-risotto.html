Step 1

Tip the rice, onion and half the butter into a large microwave-proof bowl. Cover and cook in the microwave on High for 3 mins. Stir in the stock and passata, then continue to cook, uncovered,

for 10 mins. Give it a good stir and mix in the tomatoes and mozzarella. Microwave on High

for a further 8 mins until the rice is cooked and the tomatoes have softened.

Step 2

Leave the risotto to relax for a few mins, then stir in the remaining butter,

parmesan and basil. Season to taste and serve straight from the bowl.

Tomato Risotto

Ingredients

  • 250g risotto rice
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 50g butter
  • 250ml vegetable stock
  • 500ml carton passata
  • 500g cherry tomatoes
  • 100g mozzarella
  • grated parmesan
