Step 1

Heat the oil in a medium pan, add the onion (reserving 1 tbsp to make the guacamole later) and pepper and fry, stirring frequently, for 10 mins. Stir in the garlic and spices, then tip in

the tomatoes and beans with their liquid, half a can of water and the bouillon powder.

Simmer, covered, for 15 mins.

Step 2

Meanwhile, peel and de-stone the avocado and tip into a bowl, add the remaining

onion, coriander and lime juice with a little chilli and mash well. Ladle the soup

into two bowls, top with the guacamole and serve.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 finely chopped large onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp mild chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 400g can black beans
  • 1 tsp vegetable bouillon powder
  • 1 small avocado
  • handful chopped coriander
  • 1 lime
  • 1/2 red chilli
