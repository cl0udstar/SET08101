Step 1

Bring water to boil in a medium saucepan. Add salt and butter.

Step 2

Begin stirring the water in one direction, sprinkling about 1/3 cup of the cornmeal into the depression that forms in the center. Once the water returns to the boil, pour in the rest of the cornmeal, stirring continuously to prevent clumps from forming. Turn heat

to low and continue stirring until cornmeal begins to thicken.

Step 3

Cover the pot and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, for about 10 to

15 minutes. When it is done, the mamaliga will pull away from the sides of the pot.

Step 4

Slice into six wedges and top with butter and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1-2/3 cups yellow cornmeal, medium grind
